🗳 PHASE 4: The One-Year Anniversary
CITIZENSHIP · Voting Rights · Clemency · Rights Restoration · Art. VI, § 4, Fla. Const.
Second Chance — On Casting Your Vote for the First Time in Decades
The ballot is real. It took me eight years to get there. The Safe Harbor Advisory Opinion from the Division of Elections makes it safe — get one in writing before you register. One dollar owed in LFOs makes you ineligible under Amendment 4. Clear the financial obligations first. Then vote. The clemency binder you build today is what the Governor will see in 5–7 years. Start building it today.

The Florida Felon Bill of Rights — Strategic Overview
Before Phase 1 begins, before the first P.O. meeting, before the first job application — know one foundational truth: the Florida legal system contains within it a set of rights and protections that are already yours. Not rights you must earn. Not rights you must wait for. Rights that exist now, in the statutes, and can be invoked immediately by anyone who knows they are there. The system relies on the returning citizen not knowing these rights exist. This page is the antidote to that strategy. Print it. Read it. Know it.
— Roger Pitts / Second Chance · Version 2026.1 · Curated by Devyn Mack
