🆘 EMERGENCY CONTACTS

One tap. These work even without WiFi or data.

🆘 Florida 211 — Basic Needs
Food, shelter, utilities, and crisis services. Free. 24/7 statewide. Works from any phone — no data needed.
📞 Dial 2-1-1
💬 Crisis Text Line
Mental health crisis support. Free, confidential, 24/7. Text from any carrier — no data plan required.
💬 Text HOME to 741741
🚨 911 — Life-Threatening Emergency
Police, fire, or medical emergency only. If you are in immediate danger, call now.
📞 Call 911
🧠 Mental Health & Substance Abuse
🧠 SAMHSA — Substance Abuse Helpline
Free, confidential treatment referrals. 24/7. Available in Spanish. Connects to local Florida providers.
📞 1-800-662-4357
🧬 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
Call or text 988 for mental health crisis support. Free, 24/7. Replaced the old suicide hotline number.
📞 Call or Text 988
💊 NAMI Florida — Mental Health
Support, education, and peer recovery groups for returning citizens and families. Not a crisis line — for planning and support.
📞 (850) 671-3070

Free civil legal help statewide for low-income Floridians. Housing, employment, benefits, and reentry barriers.

🗳️ FRRC — Voting Rights Restoration
Florida Rights Restoration Coalition. Free eligibility checks and LFO resolution help statewide.
